Indigenous Excellence in Agriculture

CADSEED Suppliers Limited was formed in 2009 with a vision to supply high-quality agricultural goods and services. As a 100% indigenous Malawian-owned company, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities of local farming.

Our combined practical experience and professional backgrounds ensure uncompromised procedures and project management. We don't just sell products—we build lasting relationships with farmers, agro-dealers, and stakeholders across the agricultural value chain.
